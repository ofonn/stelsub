Websites to Find Subtitles

YTS Subs

YTS Subtitles is a website that provides a vast collection of subtitles for movies and TV shows. Users can browse and download subtitles in various languages, ensuring compatibility with a wide range of media players and formats. The site is known for its extensive database, user-friendly interface, and regular updates with the latest subtitle files.

Open Subtitle

OpenSubtitles is one of the largest databases of subtitles for movies and TV shows on the internet. It offers a comprehensive collection of subtitles in multiple languages, catering to a global audience. Users can search for subtitles by movie or TV show title, browse through categories, and download subtitles in various formats. OpenSubtitles is renowned for its extensive library, user-friendly interface, and active community that contributes to subtitle uploads and translations.

Subs4free

Subs4free is one of the best sites that enables you to download free subtitle. This subtitle downloading platform is available for movies and TV series. It enables you to see top subtitles with detailed resolution.

BollyNook

BollyNook is a site to download Bollywood movie subtitles. It allows you to download subtitles in different languages, including English, Dutch, French, Arabic, etc.

English Subtitles

English Subtitles is a website having a subtitle repository for thousands of movies. It offers a dark mode UI that enables you to search for the needed title with no hassle.
